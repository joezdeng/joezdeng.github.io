About Me.

UX designer/researcher pivoting from a background in Psychology research and IT. I am taking some time to complete the Product and Design track at the Startup Institute to learn the skills to design and manage creative products. On top of that, I'm getting the network and mindset to be effective in a startup environment.

I'm Northwestern graduate in Psychology and Pre-Med with a extensive background in information technology, film production, and furniture design and construction. By continually honing an eye for aesthetics, I hope to apply my observations and experiences to define the way we interact with the digital and physical worlds. I am particularly drawn to the field of healthcare by its limitless potential for improving quality of life and its pressing need for disruption and innovation that emphasizes the users.
