About Me.

Full-stack designer pivoting from a background in Psychology research and IT with a focus on data-influenced design decisions. I am in the Product and Design track at the Startup Institute to sharpen the skills necessary to work effectively in a Startup environment.

By continually honing an eye for aesthetics, I hope to apply my observations and experiences to define the way we interact with the digital and physical worlds. I am particularly drawn to the field of healthcare by its limitless potential for improving quality of life and its pressing need for disruption and innovation that emphasizes the users.
